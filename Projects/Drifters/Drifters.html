CONCEPT

DRIFTERS is a fighting/combat game where each character uses a different genre/style for its gameplay

Miltary shooter games already demostrate something similar with their controllable tank and airplanes

I want to expand this on a way larger scale, to the point where a 2D fighter character can compete with a tank, and a strategy game character can keep an eye on the fight to steal points at the most optimal situation


BALANCING

While balancing is important, realism for weapons and attacks will make the game more fun for all players

This means that firearms should kill quickly and explosives should tear a character in half

In order to balance this these should have draw-backs like weapon jamming, being difficult to use or limiting them heavily

as an example of this, the tank character I'm currently working on has to aim the turret by spinning the left stick in the direction they want to look

For the characters that can't use these high damage weapons their movement options and awareness can be increased

However I will not be increasing their health stat unless absolutely necessary


REASON TO MAKE THIS GAME

Imagine you want to play a game with your friend, they want to play a top-down shooter, but you want to play a beat'em up, immediately your plan is ruined

I've run into this problem too many times, I'll fix it since no one else will


ADDITIONAL CONTENT

I could add campaigns to play for specific characters, some of which could be co-op

But this is a huge task, and getting the base game to work is more important so extra will happen for now
