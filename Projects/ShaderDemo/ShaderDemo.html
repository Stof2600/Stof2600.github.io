START

During one of the school years at SintLucas Eindhoven I took an interest in shader programming

All the students had to take an extra subject, these could be picked from a list

One of the subject was Software Deepening, in which you could study any software of your choosing

As long as you had a clear goal and your progress was properly documented you could work on whatever you wanted


SHADERS

I picked to work on Unity HLSL with the goal to create shaders and lighting with a similar look as Shadow of the colossus (PS2) for a single scene

While that original goal is difficult to reach within the 13 weeks we had, the end result is still pretty good

In that time I made 2 shaders and made some GPU instanced grass

The first shader is for sand, if the player walks on it it leaves a trail

The second one is a fairly basic water shader

I also spent a long time trying to get the lighting bake to be proper

At the end I had a good looking scene with an above 60FPS framerate
