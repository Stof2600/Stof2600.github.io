GENERAL INFO

Me and a friend of mine wanted to try out Godot and did a 1 week game-jam

The theme was alchemy so I used the varius symbols as cards


TECHNICAL INFO

This game uses Godot as its game engine, all assets are made by me

GAMEPLAY

On the map screen click a tile to progress

Every tile will either give you a material or process card

What a card does can be seen in the bottom left

Once reaching the end of the map you will fight against an opponent

The opponent requires a specific number of damage or higher to beat

Clicking on the material cards on the far right will add to the damage based on their level

Clicking on the process cards on the right will change some material cards, increasing their level

All Opponents have weaknesses, cards they are weak to will glow

Pressing space will end the fight

Winning will award you with an element card (top left), collect 4 to beat the game

If your damage is bellow the minimum you will take damage instead

Running out of health will cause a defeat
